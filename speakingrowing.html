speaking/rowing

In 2017, I rowed across the Atlantic in a team of four, and we still speak about it. Our team, Swiss Mocean, placed third in the 2017 race after 30 days, 4 hours, and 59 minutes at sea (and still holds the third fastest time of all time).

We speak on resilience and teamwork at companies, universities, and non-profit organizations including EY, PWC, UBS, ETH Zurich, the University of Luzern, and the juvenile detention center in Zurich. For speaking engagements please contact: info@swissmocean.ch.

The Swiss national television produced a film and 4-part documentary about our Atlantic row. If your IP is from Switzerland, you can watch it here. It's in Swiss German, but a version with English subtitles is on youtube.
